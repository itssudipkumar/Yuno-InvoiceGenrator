Brand Guidelines

Logo Usage

  • Always maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use colored version on light backgrounds
  • Use white version on dark backgrounds
  • Never rotate or distort the logo
  • Minimum size: 40px for web

Typography

  • Primary font: Inter, Segoe UI
  • Headings: Bold or Extra Bold
  • Body text: Regular weight
  • Always use proper hierarchy

Tone & Voice

  • Professional yet approachable
  • Simple and clear messaging
  • Focus on user benefits
  • Trustworthy and reliable
