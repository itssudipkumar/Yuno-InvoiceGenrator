Our Key Features

1. Save Your Invoices Automatically

Yuno saves your invoices automatically for future access, so you never have to worry about losing important documents.

2. Tag Invoices by Client Names

Easily organize invoices by tagging them with client names, making it easy to filter and search through your records.

3. Securely Store Your Business Details

Store your business details securely, and use them whenever you need to generate an invoice.

4. Accessible From Anywhere, Anytime

Yuno is cloud-based, meaning you can access your invoices from any device, anywhere, at any time.
